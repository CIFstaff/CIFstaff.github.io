Commonly Used Flurophores

Fluorophores are molecules which are capable of excitation by certain wavelengths of light at which point they emit light at a different wavelength, typically longer than excitation. Below are several common fluorophores and their excitation/emission spectra. Additional information on fluorophores not listed can be found by using Thermo Fisher's Fluorescence SpectraViewer
