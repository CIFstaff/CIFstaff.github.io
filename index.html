Princeton Confocal Microscopy Core

Princeton’s Nikon Center of Excellence was established to provide cutting-edge imaging technology to the local scientific community and to establish a mechanism for the exchange of ideas, methodologies and technologies between Princeton’s investigators and Nikon’s technology development teams. The Center enables users to investigate the dynamics of single molecules to whole organisms and tissues by providing state-of-the art confocal and super-resolution imaging systems and expert technical guidance and support from the Center’s Director, Gary Laevsky.
